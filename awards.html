Awards

2023

The first Arabic cutting-edge breast database

The mixed team headed by Pr. Amroune Mohamed published in a specialized journal the first Arabic cutting-edge breast database capable of employing ML and DL algorithms to detect and classify breast cancer. The LAMIS images have better resolution and are in full-field digital mammography format. More details.

2020

A dashboard to track coronavirus evaluation

Professor Hakim Bendjenna and his team developed the first dashboard at the provincial level for monitoring the coronavirus for the health department of the Tébessa province. Watch on YouTube | More details.

2016

First LMD doctors in Larbi Tébessi University

November 6, “Dr. Benatia Imen“ and “Dr. Afrah Djeddar” from the LAMIS laboratory became the first LMD doctors in Larbi Tébessi University, supervised by Pr. Laouar Med Rida and Pr. Hakim Bendjenna.

Best Paper Award

Best Application

Chawki Djeddi led the winning entry in the Bengali Offline Signature Verification Task competition (SigWIComp-2015) at the IEEE International Conference on Document Analysis and Recognition (ICDAR) held in Nancy, France.

Best Application

Chawki Djeddi also led the winning entry in the Writer Identification & Retrieval Task competition (SigWIComp-2015) at ICDAR 2015 in France.
