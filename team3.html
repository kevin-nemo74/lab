Team 3

  1. Analyse and Applied Mathematics

Pr. HAOUAM Kamel

head of the Analyse and Applied Mathematics team

The Analyse and Applied Mathematics team specialises in the study of:

  • Concept of dynamic system.
  • The distinction between linear systems and nonlinear systems.
  • Particular behavior that may exhibit certain nonlinear systems behavior
  • Information masking modes using chaotic systems proposed in the literature
  • Compared with the Conventional encryption.
