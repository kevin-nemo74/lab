LAMIS Databases

LAMIS-DMDB (2023)

Supervised by Amroune Mohamed: Otmani Imane, Rahmani Foued Lazhar, Soltani Hama, Benkhelifa Elhadj and Aura Conci, created LAMIS-DMDB, A new full field digital mammography database for breast cancer AI-CAD researches.

Details Access

To use the LAMIS-DMDB database, please complete and submit the Dataset Transfer Agreement.

LAMIS-MSDH (2015)

To be employed in performance evaluation, result comparison and development of new methods related to handwriting analysis and recognition, Chawki Djeddi, Abdeljalil Gattal, Labiba Souici-Meslati, Imran Siddiqi, Youcef Chibani, Haikal El Abed developed LAMIS-MSDH a new database by providing a total of 1200 text images.
