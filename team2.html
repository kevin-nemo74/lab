Team 2

  1. Distributed and Intelligent Systems

Pr. Hakim BENDJENNA

Director & head of the Distributed and Intelligent Systems team

The team is engaged with the research of distributed intelligent systems problematic in following fields:

  • Soft-computing (neural networks, genetic algorithms, fuzzy sets and fuzzy logic, rough sets).
  • Modelling and simulation of parallel and distributed systems
  • Agent and Multiagent systems.
  • Wireless Sensor Networks
