Partnership

Academic Partnerships

LAMIS has partnered with multiple academic institutions on research. The LAMIS academic partnership presents an opportunity for students to engage in a collaborative and creative process. Since 2012, LAMIS has engaged several students in its partnership with various institutions including:

  • LIUPPA/IUT of Bayonne (France)
  • Miracl Laboratory (Tunisia)
  • LIRE Laboratory (Algeria)
  • IRIT Laboratory (France)
  • Southeast Missouri State University (USA)
  • ICOSI Laboratory (Algeria)
  • University of Cape Town (South Africa)
  • RS Laboratory (Algeria)
  • University of Texas (USA)
  • ...

This cooperation has resulted in joint publications in scientific journals. Our laboratory is open to any form of cooperation: co-advising PhD students, joint research projects, conference co-organization, scientific training, and more.

Consulting

LAMIS brings science to the business of doing well. We have teams of experts that can offer valuable insight into the intricacies of the development of information technologies in several areas.

We can provide guidance on a variety of topics, including:

  • Information architecture and data models
  • Application and website development
  • Workflow analysis
  • Security systems
